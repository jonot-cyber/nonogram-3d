Nonogram 3D

Picross 3D was a video game released in 2009 for the Nintendo DS. Some people (me) would consider it to be one of the best games of all time. Unfortunately, due to the age of this game and the hardware, the game is not very accessible to most people. In addition, the Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection shut down in 2014, so the online functionality of the game no longer works.

My goal with this project is to remake the game for web browsers, allowing anyone to play the game, along with make and share their own puzzles.
